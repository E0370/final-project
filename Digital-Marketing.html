Digital Marketing

Digital Garage

Platform:

Google Digital Garage Why it’s great: This free course is perfect for beginners and provides a strong foundation in digital marketing. Since it's from Google, it covers industry-standard tools and strategies. Key topics: SEO, SEM (Search Engine Marketing), social media marketing, content marketing, and more. Duration: 40 hours, with certification upon completion. Best for: Beginners who want to understand all the key aspects of digital marketing

Hubspot Academy

Platform:

HubSpot Academy Why it’s great: HubSpot is a leader in inbound marketing, and this course provides a deep dive into content marketing, which is a critical part of any digital marketing strategy. Key topics: Content creation, SEO strategy, social media content, and analytics. Duration: 6 hours, with certification upon completion. Best for: Students who want to specialize in content marketing and learn to attract customers with effective content.

Semrush Academy

Platform:

SEMrush Academy Why it’s great: SEMrush is a powerful tool used by professionals, and this free course offers expert knowledge on how to master SEO. It's very practical and highly respected in the industry. Key topics: Keyword research, on-page SEO, technical SEO, and backlink building. Duration: 4 hours, with certification. Best for: Students who want to specialize in SEO and learn how to drive organic traffic through search engines
